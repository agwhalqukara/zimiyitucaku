Terry Hohm Identified as the Victim of Fatal I-40 West Crash in Knoxville

In a tragic incident that occurred on Thursday, the victim of a fatal crash on Interstate 40 West has been identified as Terry Hohm, aged 64, from Snellville, Georgia.

The accident, currently under investigation by the Knoxville Police Department, involved a tire crashing through the windshield of Hohm’s vehicle. The unexpected event led to a severe collision, resulting in Hohm’s untimely passing.

Terry Hohm was traveling westbound on I-40 when the tire, presumably from another vehicle, breached his windshield. Despite emergency response efforts, Hohm succumbed to injuries sustained during the crash.

The incident has left the community shocked and saddened. As news of Hohm’s death spread, condolences began pouring in from those who knew him, as well as from those who were moved by the tragic circumstances surrounding his passing.

Hailing from Snellville, Georgia, Hohm was known for his kindness and amiable nature. His sudden and tragic loss has deeply affected his family, friends, and the wider community.

The Knoxville Police Department continues its investigation into the accident, aiming to piece together the events leading up to the fatal incident. It’s still unclear where the tire originated from and how it ended up crashing through Hohm’s windshield.

This tragic incident serves as a grim reminder of the unpredictability of road travel and the importance of vehicle safety. Our thoughts are with the family and friends of Terry Hohm during this difficult time.

Lady Gagas Dog Thieves Arrested: 5 Caught and Linked to the Crime

- Lady Gaga's dog walker was attacked and her dogs were taken just two months ago

- Five people have been arrested and Gaga’s fur babies, Koji and Gusta, are home safely

- One of the ladies involved in the shooting and stealing of Gaga’s French Bulldogs returned her dogs and helped close the case

Lady Gaga has gotten justice for her dog walker who was attacked not too long ago. Five people have been arrested and Gaga’s fur babies, Koji and Gusta, are home safely.

It has been two months since the incident. One of the ladies involved in the shooting and stealing of Gaga’s French Bulldogs returned her dogs and helped close the case – she was one of the five who were arrested.

Los Angeles police claimed that the men who confronted the singer's dog walker - James Jackson, 18, Jaylin White, 19, and Lafayette Whaley, 27, did not know the French Bulldogs belonged to Gaga.

District Attorney George Gascón labelled the crime as ‘bizarre’.

"This was a brazen street crime that left a man seriously wounded."

Detectives said that Jennifer McBride, the woman who returned the dog, was involved as she was in a relationship with one of the boy’s fathers. Jennifer was charged with accessory to the crime.

Gaga had put out a whopping $500 000 (about R7 million) reward for her fur babies. Gaga was in Italy at the time of the crime. Gaga is glad that her dogs are home safely and that the perpetrators have been caught.

Briefly News previously reported that American celebrity Lady Gaga’s dog walker is on the road to recovery. The ailing man was shot four times in the chest during a robbery and dognapping that occurred in Hollywood.

A source close to the musician told People magazine that Ryan Fischer had been critically wounded during the scuffle but seems to be getting better.

It was also reported that pop icon Lady Gaga performed the US national anthem at Joe Biden's inauguration and according to the reactions online, she definitely killed it.

The star, who is known for her over-the-top outfits, did not disappoint as she showed up at the podium in the US Capitol, wearing a custom Schiaparelli Haute Couture outfit.

The dress had a billowing red bottom and a form-fitting top in black. A golden dove brooch was also added to her outfit and this instantly made fans describe it as being an outfit like those from the film series of The Hunger Games.

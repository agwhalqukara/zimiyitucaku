Chris Brown Lands In Trouble For This TikTok Video That He Did With A Famous Person Who Made Racist

Chris Brown’s recent attempt to take over TikTok’s scene was met with waves of backlash from fans, as the singer shared a video clip in which he dances in the company of Tyga and the controversial YouTuber, Austin McBroom.

In the footage, Tyga and McBroom can be seen standing in the front and nodding their heads to the rhythm of “U Can’t Touch This” by MC Hammer, while Brown puts his envious dancing skills on display behind his friends.

Fans were immediately smitten by Brown’s dance, and many praised him for his moves, as one follower wrote “ooo I’m living for thissss” and lots of others filled the comment section with heart emojis.

However, even though the majority of the public found it charming that the “No Guidance” lyricist was self-isolating with his fellow colleague, many others were concerned that the rapper was so close to Austin.

The YouTube star was recently condemned for being insensitive and a racist, mainly due to his messages on social media, such as “Can’t do black girls cuz after I’m done, imma have to pay for their hair” or “St. Louis is scaring me away from black girls…..blondes with blue eyes are looking more appealing.”

One follower said: “Can Austin please move out of the way? He’s cool and all, but he doesn’t need to be there. 😂”

This person stated: “It had to be said, thank you 😂.” Another critic shared: “Nah, sis, he’s disrespectful to black women.”

Meanwhile, Brown recently made headlines when it was revealed that the R&B crooner could not see his infant son, Aeko Brown, due to the ongoing Coronavirus crisis.

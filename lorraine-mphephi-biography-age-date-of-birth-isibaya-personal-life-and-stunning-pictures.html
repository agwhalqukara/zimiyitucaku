Lorraine Mphephi biography: age, date of birth, Isibaya, personal life and stunning pictures

Lorraine Mphephi who is famously known as Lolo Mphephi is a star known for her acting. Many will remember her for the role she played on Yizo Yizo, an SABC1 drama series. She starred as Mantwa and was part of the leading actresses in the show from 1999 to 2004.

In addition to that, the actress also played the role of Pam, a stubborn maid in Zungu's household. She was Iris favourite on isiBaya, a soapie that airs on Mzansi Magic. She has also been a guest star on other shows including Home Affairs, a drama series on SABC1, Zero Tolerance and Tshisa; Single Guys which is a SABC1 sitcom, Mtunzini.com, aYeYe which is a Vuzu Amp drama series and on Umlilo, the e.tv drama series.

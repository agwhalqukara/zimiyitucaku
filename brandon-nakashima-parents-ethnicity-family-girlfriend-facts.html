Brandon Nakashima is an American tennis player who now ranks world No. 49 in singles by the Association of Tennis Professionals. Moreover, he ranks world No. 409 in men’s doubles.

Brandon Nakashima Parents & Family Life

Nakashima was born to parents Wesley Nakashima and Christina Nakashima on August 3, 2001. He hails from San Diego, California, United States.


Both his father and mother are pharmacists by profession. His father Wesley is an ICU clinical pharmacist and his mother Christina is a pharmacy supervisor.


Brandon grew up with a younger brother named Bryce Nakashima. Bryce, age 18, is also a tennis player who now ranks #913 in the ATP doubles ranking.

What Is Brandon Nakashima’s Ethnicity?

Nakashima is Asian by ethnicity as both his parents are of Asian heritage. His father was born in California and is of Japanese ancestry.

His Career Journey

Kendall And Kylie Jenner At War! Supermodel Is Reportedly Jealous Of The Lip kit Moguls Massive F

The sibling rivalry in the Kardashian-Jenner family has reached a new level. According to a new report, Kendall and Kylie Jenner are at odds over money, and the sisters can barely talk to each other.

“Kendall figures it’s crazy how much Kylie’s worth [an estimated $900M] while she’s only got a fraction of that [approx. $50M] in the bank herself,” an insider recently told Radar Online.

The source went on to say that Kendall and Kylie have nothing in common, and things really started to fall apart between them when Kylie launched her makeup line, and it turned out to be uber-successful.

The fact that the family considered Kendall’s battle with acne and partnership with Proactive a bombshell personal confession led to a ton of ridicule on social media, with fans calling it a “pathetic cry for attention.”

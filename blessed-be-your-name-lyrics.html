Blessed be your name lyrics

“Blessed Be Your Name” is a popular contemporary worship song written by Matt Redman and Beth Redman.

The song was first released in 2002 as part of the album “The Worship Collection.”

The song’s lyrics are based on the biblical story of Job, who despite losing everything he had, continued to bless God.

It encourages listeners to continue to praise God even in difficult circumstances.

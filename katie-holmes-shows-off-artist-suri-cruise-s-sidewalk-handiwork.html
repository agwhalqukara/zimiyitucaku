Katie Holmes Shows Off &quot;Artist&quot; Suri Cruise's Sidewalk Handiwork

Look what I can do, mommy! Katie Holmes was one proud mama showing off her little girl Suri Cruise’s sidewalk art in an Instagram snap on Friday, Sept. 4.

“My artist creating with me,” the All We Had actress captioned the adorable photo, in which Suri, 9, can be seen crouching over a giant wooden panel and using red spray-paint to draw a hopscotch board.

In the photo, the Hollywood youngster, whose dad is Tom Cruise, is wearing an adult’s red shirt backwards as a smock and pink-and-green ballet flats with her hair pulled back in a high bun.

The mother-daughter duo are often having fun wherever they go. Earlier this year, Holmes helped her little girl celebrate her ninth birthday with a colorful bash at the Gilded Lily.

“Katie got an assortment of vanilla, chocolate, and red velvet cupcakes decorated in pink and purple from Georgetown Cupcake SoHo,” an insider told Us of the April 18 bash. “Dozens and dozens of cupcakes were placed on cupcake towers for the celebration!”

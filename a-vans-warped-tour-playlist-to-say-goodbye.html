A Vans Warped Tour Playlist to Say &quot;Goodbye&quot;

It’s 2018 and it’s time for us to say goodbye to Vans Warped Tour.

This iconic tour, which has been around since 1995, is having its final run this year. But even though we’re so sad to see it go, we can’t help but cry happy tears over this final line-up.

Do you see how beautiful it is? Do you see how wonderfully thought out this is? And we don’t know about you, but we’re seriously considering buying a ticket to every single city, just so we can hear this line-up every single day for an entire summer.

Lots of our favorites are a part of this line-up. We’re also glad to see that some of the bands that started off here get to be a part of its final run.

And here’s a playlist of some of our favorite artists – it’s a bit empty, so feel free to keep a copy for yourself and add on to it.

We’re so sad to see Warped Tour go, but we’re also super excited to see all these bands in one day. We’re sort of conflicted on how to feel about letting this go. Especially when some of us have grown up with Warped Tour.

We’d like to thank Vans for having put this on every summer – we cannot begin to express how thankful we are for allowing people all across the country meet and see some of their favorite artists (especially when these shows are happening in cities that don’t usually get some of these artists during their regular tour dates).

Are you going to Warped Tour? What date(s)? And who are you most excited to see?

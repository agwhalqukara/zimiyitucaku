Kendall Jenner Towers Over Sisters Kim, Khloe Kardashian: Photos

Who’s the baby now? 

One can only imagine all the menial tasks Kendall Jenner was likely subjected to growing up as one of the younger sisters in the Kardashian-Jenner household — oh, but the tables have finally turned.

Hours after launching their Kardashian Beauty hair product line, the Estee Lauder spokesmodel, 19, and her older half-sisters Kim and Khloe Kardashian, attended the launch party of family friend Simon Huck‘s new Command Entertainment Group in New York City, on Tuesday, Feb. 10. The statuesque 5-feet, 10-inches tall beauty made her big sister Kim Kardashian look like a little minion.

Jenner stole the spotlight in a sultry Celine sweaterdress with two extremely high side slits and black leather thigh-high boots.

Still, the older sisters were just as captivating: Kim, 34, looked smoking-hot in a cream two-piece strapless crop top and maxi skirt ensemble that featured a split in the center, while showing off her new shoulder-length haircut.

Lawson channel 80s vibes on new single 'Animals' -CelebMix

Hot on the heels of their comeback single ‘Lovers’, Lawson are back with a brand new track called ‘Animals’.

As they continue to experiment with their sound, ‘Animals’ sees Lawson incorporate 80s vibes into their trademark anthemic style. Armed with an euphoric chorus, which will no doubt sound sensational when performed live, ‘Animals’ is a track which wouldn’t be out of place on a The 1975 or LANY record.

Listen to ‘Animals’ below.

Both ‘Animals’ and ‘Lovers’ mark the beginning of an exciting new chapter for Lawson, who first made their debut in 2012 with ‘Standing In The Dark’. We’re loving what we’ve heard so far from their releases this time around, and with more new music on the horizon, Andy Brown, Joel Peat, Ryan Fletcher and Adam Pitts are bringing a little bit of sunshine to 2020.

For more Lawson content, check out our article about five of their best singles.

‘Animals’ is available to download and stream now.

RICK ROSS EX-GIRLFRIEND BRIANA CAMILLE SUES FOR PATERNITY OF THEIR KIDS

Rick Ross is being sued by the mother of two of his children. The celebrity father’s ex-girlfriend, Briana Camille, filed documents with the courts in the hope of establishing paternity for their kids, Berkeley and Billion.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B6idrTmlcm7/

According to BOSSIP,

“Briana Camille recently sued her rapper ex for paternity and to establish both temporary and permanent child support for their two children. Briana also dropped another bombshell in the court documents; she’s expecting their third child in the fall, according to her complaint.”

Briana Camille and Rick Ross reportedly lived together for two years before deciding to end their relationship near the end of last year. Briana allegedly decided to move out of the house with her and Rick’s children. Ross, however, has reportedly supported his ex-girlfriend and their kids ever since Camille decided to move on.

It is because of his financial support that Rick Ross wants the paternity case thrown out. The celebrity father allegedly feels it unnecessary for him to undergo the procedures to officially establish him as Billion and Berkeley’s father since he has never denied his children. Of course, establishing paternity also comes with the attachment of child support, which Camille reportedly wants to establish as well.

Briana Camille and Rick Ross’ third child will make him a father for the fifth time. Stay tuned for more celebrity dad news!

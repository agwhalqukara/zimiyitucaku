Dexter Tiewon Gore Jr., aka Famous Dex, recently shared an Instagram post about meeting his girlfriend's mother and father for the first time. Yes, he is not single in 2023. The 29-Year-Old rapper, however, has yet to reveal anything about his relationship or the identity of his new gf or let's say, his to-be-wife. 

Dexter Tiewon Gore Jr. (born September 6, 1993) is an American rapper and singer better known by his stage name Famous Dex. His debut album Dex Meets Dexter debuted at number 12 on the Billboard 200 in 2018, while its singles Pick It Up (featuring ASAP Rocky) and Japan debuted at number 54 and 28 on the Billboard Hot 100, respectively.

Famous Dex. was born in Manhattan's Harlem neighborhood but had since moved to Chicago, Illinois, and was raised in the city's Englewood neighborhood on the city's South Side. Later, he decided to pursue music after his mother died in September 2014. He got a pink ribbon tattooed on his face in 2016 to honor his mother, who died of breast cancer.

Prior to the University of California, Irvine's Shocktoberfest music event in 2018, Student Services was chastised for allowing Gore to perform after a security video from two years prior surfaced, showing Gore chasing his girlfriend down a hallway and then hitting her several times. Despite the domestic abuse revelations, Gore was allowed to perform, and while doing so, he jumped into the crowded audience, knocking over safety barriers in the process.

Famous Dex’s Girlfriend/Wife in 2023: The 29-Year-Old Rapper Is Currently in a Relationship With His Unidentified Partner, However, No Details (Including Her Name) Have Not Been Revealed Yet!

As of 2023, Famous Dex (@famousdex) does not have a wife as he has never been married, but it seems the rapper is in a serious relationship right now. Meanwhile, the name or any detail of his girlfriend has not been made public yet. However, the rapper recently revealed on his Instagram story that he had met the parents of his girlfriend for the first time. Despite the fact that he did not reveal her identity, his post confirms that he is currently dating an unidentified girl.

Despite the 29-year-old rapper's efforts to hide his girlfriend's identity, Dex has revealed some details about their relationship on social media. Recently, on Valentine's Day in 2023, he posted a video to his Instagram story in which he revealed that his girlfriend had surprised him with a beautifully decorated room filled with rose flowers. Dex captioned the video, "Wow, this is beautiful, I'm beyond blessed," expressing his gratitude for her gesture. It's unclear how long they've been dating, but Dex has been in at least one relationship before.

Famous Dex has previously gained attention for all the wrong reasons and has been involved in numerous controversies. Meanwhile, Dex was caught on security footage in September 2016 viciously hitting his fiancée in the hallway of a Hollywood condominium.

Julia Roberts Debuts Pretty Woman-Red Hair at 2015 SAG Awards: Photos

Pretty Woman—once and forever!

Julia Roberts attended the 2015 SAG Awards in L.A. on Jan. 25, where she debuted a fierce—and very familiar—hair hue: Vivian Ward red!

PHOTOS: Everyone at the 2015 SAGs!

The Normal Heart actress, 47, nominated for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Television Move, popped on the red carpet thanks to her rust-hued tresses—nearly an identical match to the reddish brown hair color she rocked as everyone’s favorite big-hearted hooker in 1990’s Pretty Woman.

To complement her new hue, celeb makeup artist Genevieve Herr kept the beauty look simple yet classic with a smokey eye and nude lip. She created the ’40s-inspired makeup with Lancome Color Design 5-Pan Eyeshadow Palette in Midnight Rush and the new Lancome Color Design Sensational Effects Lipcolor in Haute Nude.

But that wasn’t the only thing that helped her sparkle! The mom of three—to twins Hazel and Phinnaeus, 10, and Henry, 7—accessorized her sleek and chic Givenchy jumpsuit with $20,000 diamond drop earrings, an $18,000 diamond ring, and a $280,000 diamond and platinum bracelet, all courtesy of Tiffany & Co. 

And yet, despite her dazzling accessories, Roberts’ trademark smile shined even brighter, thanks to her costar, Mark Ruffalo‘s win for The Normal Heart. When she took the stage to present the award for Outstanding Male Actor in a Leading Role, the Oscar winner stopped to gush, “I just wanted to say that Mark Ruffalo is one outstanding actor. And I am just tickled to my toes that he won tonight.”

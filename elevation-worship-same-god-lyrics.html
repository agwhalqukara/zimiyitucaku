About

Elevation Worship is a contemporary Christian music band from Elevation Church in Charlotte, North Carolina. The group formed in 2007 and has since released multiple albums and singles that have charted on Billboard’s Christian music charts.

Some of their most well-known songs include “O Come to the Altar,” “Here as in Heaven,” and “Graves Into Gardens.” Elevation Worship is known for its passionate and energetic live performances, and is considered one of the most influential worship bands in modern Christian music.

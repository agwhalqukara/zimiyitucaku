Outcast Zende Steals Luna, Becomes New Forrester Villain

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers tease that bad boy Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson) is rehabilitated via the love of Hope Logan (Annika Noelle).

He has not gaslighted his son, nor kissed a Hope Mannequin, in years, and let’s hope it stays this way. But every soap needs a villain, and if it’s not Thomas, then who is worthy of taking his mantle?

And by the way, while we’re conjecturing, WHY is the show not using OG bad boy Bill Spencer (Don Diamont) these days?

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers: Who is The Show’s Next Bad Boy?

If neither Thomas nor Bill is going to run amok and cause trouble in LA, perhaps Zende Forrester (Delon deMetz) could take on that role?

The show seems to be pulling him in that direction, with Eric Forrester’s (John McCook) situation showing him how little his family cares about him.

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers: Will Zende Turn Dark?

Zende was left out of the loop about Eric’s deadly condition, and he was snubbed in favor of RJ Forrester (Joshua Hoffman) when Eric wanted a design partner.

Zende is pushing back, questioning Ridge Forrester’s (Thorsten Kaye) failure to include him in visits to the hospital and whining about RJ being chosen to finish Eric’s design line.

Zende has a point, he is a professional designer and RJ’s claim to fame is being an influencer. It would hurt Zende to see his talent pushed aside for an inexperienced newbie, and Ridge’s decision smacks of nepotism.

B&B spoilers: Eric Forrester’s Dilemma

Zende and Eric have collaborated in the past but Eric chose RJ to help him after learning he is dying. And, when Ridge could have told Zende about Eric, he told Carter Walton (Lawrence Saint-Victor) first because he’s “family.”

This is not the first time Ridge has been a jerk, remember when he threw out Eric’s treasured stapler and told him to just play pickleball already?

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers: Will Zende Turn on His Family?

Zende is whining to Luna Nozawa (Lisa Yamada) about his situation and Luna has been very sympathetic. Plus the show has made it a point to portray Luna as being like her loose mom Poppy Nozawa (Romy Park).

Is the writing on the wall B&B fans, is Zende about to betray his family by taking his cousin’s girl?
